# Sonic Sommelier — Demo Video Script (3 minutes)

**Format:** Screen recording with AI voiceover via Kite.video
**Tone:** Warm, confident, slightly reverent — like a food documentary narrator

---

## SCENE 1: Hook (0:00–0:20)

**Screen:** Black screen, then the landing page fades in — dark, elegant, album art floating. Maybe a quick cut of a finished experience: a dish image, a wine pairing, a track playing.

**Voiceover:**
"You press play on a Herbie Mann record. The flute is warm, humid, unhurried. Now imagine an AI that hears that warmth — and turns it into miso-glazed radishes with sesame. Pairs it with a Gruner Veltliner. Writes you the recipe. Narrates the whole evening like a late-night radio host serving you dinner. That's Sonic Sommelier — four Mistral AI agents that turn the feeling of music into a 5-course dining experience you can actually cook tonight."

---

## SCENE 2: The Problem (0:20–0:35)

**Screen:** Quick montage — a Spotify playlist on one side, a recipe app on the other, disconnected

**Voiceover:**
"Music sets the mood for a meal. But no one has ever connected them intelligently. We asked: what if AI could hear a song the way a chef tastes a dish — feeling the texture, the weight, the cultural roots — and translate that into food, wine, and a complete evening? Not a playlist recommendation. Not a recipe search. A full sensory experience designed by AI agents that actually understand the connection."

---

## SCENE 3: Meet the Maitre D' (0:35–0:55)

**Screen:** Click "New Experience." Show the chat interface with the prompt suggestions visible. Click "Flute jazz on a rainy night."

**Voiceover:**
"It starts with a conversation. You tell the Maitre D' what you're in the mood for — an artist, a genre, even a specific instrument. Here, we're saying 'flute jazz on a rainy night.' No menus, no dropdowns — just natural language."

**Screen:** Show the message being sent. The response appears: "Your table is being prepared..."

**Voiceover:**
"The Maitre D' is our first Mistral agent. Built on the Mistral Agents API, it interprets your input and generates a creative brief — the mood, the cuisine direction, and a poetic title for your evening. That brief drives the entire pipeline."

---

## SCENE 4: The Four-Agent Pipeline (0:55–1:45)

**Screen:** Show the loading state with agent thoughts streaming in real-time

**Voiceover:**
"Now four Mistral AI agents take over, working in sequence. You can watch each one thinking in real time."

**Screen:** Highlight "Building your sonic journey..." thought bubble, then track names appearing

**Voiceover:**
"Agent two — the Music Curator — searches Spotify for five real tracks that form a dining arc. From a delicate amuse-bouche opener to an emotional main course peak to a gentle dessert close. But here's the key innovation: for each track, the curator writes what we call a sonic fingerprint — a natural language description of how the music feels. 'Liquid flute lines floating over a Memphis funk groove, humid and unhurried.' That sonic fingerprint is what the next agents read to design food and wine."

**Screen:** Highlight "Translating sound into flavor..." thought bubble, then course details appearing with ingredient counts

**Voiceover:**
"Agent three — the Culinary Chef — reads those sonic fingerprints and translates them into dishes. A humid bossa nova groove becomes citrus and cilantro. A deep, brooding bassline becomes braised short ribs. And every dish comes with a complete recipe — real ingredients with measurements, step-by-step instructions, prep times — all generated by Mistral. No recipe API. The AI designs recipes that precisely match the music."

**Screen:** Highlight wine pairing thoughts appearing

**Voiceover:**
"Agent four — the Wine Sommelier. Its system prompt contains 650 lines of wine expertise mapping every sonic dimension to a beverage. High energy gets bold Barolo. Delicate acoustics get crisp Gruner Veltliner. The sommelier also calls Spoonacular's wine API as a tool — getting real bottle recommendations, price ranges, and wine descriptions — then synthesizes that data with its sonic mapping to write tasting notes that connect the wine, the dish, and the music."

---

## SCENE 5: The Experience — Story Flow (1:45–2:25)

**Screen:** The experience loads. Tap through the story flow screens.

**Voiceover:**
"When the pipeline completes, you get this — a tap-through experience inspired by Spotify Wrapped."

**Screen:** Show the title screen with the generated palette

**Voiceover:**
"Every experience gets a unique color palette derived algorithmically from the music's sonic profile. The UI literally looks like the music sounds."

**Screen:** Tap to the Sonic Profile radar chart

**Voiceover:**
"Your sonic profile — energy, valence, tempo, danceability, acousticness — visualized as a radar chart."

**Screen:** Tap to a course card — the YouTube track starts playing in the background, then ElevenLabs narration plays over it

**Voiceover:**
"Now watch this. Each course card shows AI-generated dish photography, the paired track with album art — and the actual song starts playing in the background via YouTube. Then, layered on top —"

**Screen:** Let the ElevenLabs narration play for 5-8 seconds with the music audible underneath it

**(PAUSE voiceover — let the app speak for itself. The audience hears the music playing with the ElevenLabs narration over it.)**

**Voiceover (after the narration clip):**
"That's ElevenLabs V3. The narration blends a late-night radio host with a fine-dining waiter — naming the dish, the track, and the wine — while the actual song plays underneath. It's like having a personal DJ and sommelier narrating your dinner."

**Screen:** Show the wine pairing section on the course card

**Voiceover:**
"Each wine pairing comes with a tasting note that weaves together all three senses — how the wine tastes, how it interacts with the dish, and how it mirrors the sonic profile of the track."
